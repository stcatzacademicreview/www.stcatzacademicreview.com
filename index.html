About Us

St Catherine’s Academic Review (SCAR) is a student led, interdisciplinary journal publishing book reviews, research articles, and essays in the mathematical, physical, and life sciences (MPLS), social science, and humanities.

Our aim is to highlight the academic excellence and innovation available from the members of St Catherine’s College, and share it on an open access, peer reviewed platform. As such, all our published articles are freely available online, and articles are published continually as they are reviewed and accepted.

For members or alumni of St Catz interested in publishing an article with us, please see our guidelines page for more details or submit an abstract below.

General Guidelines for Authors

We accept articles from both members and alumni of St Catherine’s College, Oxford to be published in SCAR. If you are looking to publish with us, we ask that your write in the Chicago style with regards to grammar and mechanics. Please see our guidelines document for more details.

MPLS Guidelines

For authors submitting articles in the mathematics, physical, and life sciences, we ask that you pick a writing format and citating style relevant and appropriate to your discipline/topic. In cases where there are differencse with the Chicago style regarding grammar and mechanics only, use the Chicago style. Please see our guidelines document for more details.

Social Sciences Guidelines

For authors submitting articles in the social sciences, we ask that you write and reference in APA style. In cases where there are differences with the Chicago style regarding grammar and mechanics only, use the Chicago style. Please consult APA guidelines and see our guidelines document for more details.
